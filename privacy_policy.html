GDPR (EU/EEA Users)

For users in the EU/EEA/UK/Switzerland, we process data under GDPR. Lawful basis: Consent for personalized ads.

  • Data Processed: Device identifiers (Advertising ID), IP for ads.
  • Rights: Access, rectification, erasure ("right to be forgotten"), restriction, objection, portability. No automated decisions/profiling.
  • Withdraw Consent: Toggle in-app settings or email KidBackStudios@gmail.com. Rejection = generic ads only.
  • Data Transfers: To US providers (Unity/ironSource) under Standard Contractual Clauses.
  • Contact for requests: KidBackStudios@gmail.com (response within 1 month).

CCPA/CPRA (California Residents)

For CA residents (or if we process 50K+ CA users/year), we comply with CCPA/CPRA. We do not "sell" data but share for ads (considered "sharing" under CPRA).

  • Categories Shared: Identifiers (Advertising ID) with ad providers.
  • Rights: Know (disclosure), delete, opt-out of sale/share (no discrimination). Exercise via "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link in-app or email KidBackStudios@gmail.com.
  • Opt-out honored globally.

COPPA (Children's Privacy)

The App is not directed to children under 13 but may appeal to mixed audiences. We comply with COPPA.

  • Age Screening: In-app gate asks "over 13?" Under-13: No personalized ads; flagged child-directed (generic ads only).
  • No Collection from Children: No knowing collection without verifiable parental consent (neutral screen used). If discovered, delete immediately.
  • Parental Rights: Contact KidBackStudios@gmail.com to review/delete child's data.

Third-party ads (LevelPlay) comply when flagged.

iOS App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS 14.5+, we request ATT permission for IDFA (Advertising ID) to personalize ads. Denied = no IDFA; generic ads only. Manage in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking. See Apple's ATT Info.
